About Me

I'm a computer science major with a deep passion for cars and motorcycles. Ever since I was young, I’ve been fascinated by how things work—whether it’s engines, software, or anything in between. That curiosity led me to pursue computer science, where I get to solve complex problems and create innovative solutions. Outside of coding, you’ll often find me working on cars, riding motorcycles, or learning about the latest advancements in automotive technology.

I love the combination of engineering and design that goes into making vehicles perform at their best. Whether it’s the thrill of a fast ride or the satisfaction of fixing something with my own hands, cars and motorcycles are a huge part of my life. As I approach graduation, I’m excited about the opportunities ahead.

I can’t wait to apply my technical skills in a field that challenges me and keeps me learning. Whether it’s software development, AI, or even automotive tech, I’m eager to find a career that blends my love for technology and mechanics. For now, I’m enjoying the journey, learning as much as I can, and looking forward to the road ahead—both in my career and on the open road.
